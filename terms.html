Cookies Policy – The Tiny Flavor Trackers

Like a sprinkle of red pepper flakes, cookies enhance your experience on Yttrium Flavor Alchemy. This policy explains what cookies are and how we use them.

What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device to remember preferences and collect anonymous usage data. They make the site work efficiently.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site navigation, security, and login (if we later implement user accounts). Without them, the Crimson Lab would glitch.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your favorite diet adaptation selections, metric/imperial toggle, and recipe view settings.
  • Analytics Cookies: We use lightweight, self-hosted analytics or cookieless tracking to count visits and popular recipes. No cross-site tracking or Google Analytics invasive scripts.
  • Functionality Cookies: Enable embedded video players and basic social sharing (without selling data).

We do NOT use advertising cookies or any behavioral tracking for targeting ads. Our kitchen is ad-free and proud.

3. Your Cookie Choices

You can manage or delete cookies via your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect site performance (e.g., search filters may reset). Most browsers allow you to block third-party cookies entirely. For specific instructions, see aboutcookies.org.

4. Consent Management

When you first visit our site, you see a simple cookie banner allowing you to accept only essential cookies or enable analytics/preferences. Your choice is stored for 6 months. You can change preferences anytime via the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

🍅 No cookie crumb left behind: We do not use cookies for retargeting, nor do we share data with any advertising networks. Our alchemy is pure, just like your pantry.

For any cookie-related questions, contact our data protection alchemist at dpo@yttriumflavor.com.
