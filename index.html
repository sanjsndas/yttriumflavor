🔥 Ruby Yttrium Tomato Bouillabaisse

Prep: 20min | Cook: 45min | Serves: 6

Ingredients:

  • 2 lb ripe heirloom tomatoes (reddest you can find)
  • 1 lb shrimp, tail-on
  • 1 lb mussels, scrubbed
  • 1 bulb fennel, thinly sliced
  • 4 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 3 cups fish stock
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp saffron threads
  • Salt, smoked paprika, red pepper flakes

Method:

  1. In a large red enamel pot, sauté fennel and garlic until fragrant.
  2. Add tomato paste and cook until brick-red, 2 min.
  3. Deglaze with white wine, reduce by half.
  4. Add tomatoes (crushed), stock, saffron, and spices. Simmer 30 min.
  5. Add seafood in last 8 minutes until mussels open and shrimp turn pink.
  6. Serve with crusty bread and a drizzle of chili oil. A burst of oceanic umami meets tomato depth — a true alchemical creation.

Chef’s note: Yttrium Alchemy suggests using cast-iron for mineral infusion. This dish delivers 34g protein per serving and high lycopene.

🍷 Merlot-Braised "Elemental" Short Ribs

Prep: 30min + marinate | Cook: 3h | Serves: 4

Ingredients:

  • 4 lbs beef short ribs, bone-in
  • 2 cups dry Merlot
  • 2 red onions, chopped
  • 4 carrots, large dice
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 3 sprigs rosemary + thyme
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste

Method:

  1. Season ribs heavily with salt and pepper. Sear in batches until deep mahogany.
  2. Add onions, carrots, garlic — cook 5 min.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, cook 2 min. Deglaze with Merlot, scraping fond.
  4. Return ribs to pot, add broth and herbs. Cover and braise at 325°F for 2.5–3h until fork-tender.
  5. Skim fat, reduce braising liquid to a silky red wine glaze.
  6. Serve over parsnip purée. The alchemy of slow cooking transforms collagen into velvet.

Pair with a bold Syrah. Each bite resonates with Yttrium’s signature: transformation through heat and patience.

🌶️ Spiced Red Lentil & Yttrium Ras el Hanout

Time: 40min | Vegan

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups red lentils, rinsed
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes (14oz)
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp Ras el Hanout
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 4 cups vegetable stock
  • Juice of 1 lemon

Method:

  1. Sauté onion, garlic until translucent. Add spices, bloom 1 min.
  2. Add lentils, tomatoes, stock. Bring to simmer.
  3. Cook 25 min until lentils break down into a creamy red stew.
  4. Finish with lemon, fresh cilantro, and a dollop of coconut yogurt.
  5. Serve with warm flatbread. This earthy, iron-rich soup represents the grounding side of flavor alchemy.
